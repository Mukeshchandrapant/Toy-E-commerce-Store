# Toy-E-commerce-Store
📌 Overview
This project is a Business Intelligence (BI) dashboard designed to analyze the performance of a toy e-commerce store. It provides actionable insights into sales, refunds, customer behavior, website traffic, and product intelligence. The goal is to help stakeholders make data-driven decisions that improve revenue, customer retention, and marketing ROI.

💼 Business Questions

📦 Revenue & Sales Performance
- What is total revenue, COGS, and gross profit, Net profit by month/quarter/year?
- Which products generate the highest revenue and margin?
- What is the average order value (AOV) trend over time?
- Average Order quantity per order?
- Which is the top-selling primary product?
  
🔄 Refunds & Returns Analysis
- What is the refund rate (% of orders refunded)?
- What is the total refund value by product, month, or channel?
- Which products have the highest return rates?
- What is the net revenue after refunds?
- Are refund rates increasing or decreasing over time? Total orders VS Returned order for Financial year.
 
🌐 Website & Traffic Analysis
- How many sessions and pageviews does the site receive daily/weekly?
- What is the session-to-order conversion rate?
- Which traffic source (utm_source) drives the most revenue?
- Which campaigns (utm_campaign) have the best ROI?
- What is the bounce rate by device type (mobile vs desktop)?
- Which referrer (http_referer) brings the highest-value customers?

🔁 Customer Behaviour
- What % of sessions are repeat visitors (is_repeat_session)?
- Do repeat visitors convert at a higher rate than new visitors?
- What is the customer purchase frequency?
  
📱 Device & Channel Analysis
- Does device type (mobile/tablet/desktop) affect conversion rate?

🏆 Product Intelligence
- What is the attach rate — how often is a product bought as a secondary item vs primary?
- Which product combinations are frequently purchased together?
- What is the COGS-to-revenue ratio per product (margin health)?
- Which products drive the most refunds by value?

🛠️ Tech Stack
- Power BI / DAX → Interactive dashboards & measures
- Excel → Data cleaning & quick analysis

🚀 Key Insights
- Identify top-performing products and optimize inventory.
- Track conversion rates across traffic sources and campaigns.
- Monitor refund trends to improve product quality and customer satisfaction.
- Segment customers by lifetime value to prioritize retention strategies.
- Understand device and channel performance for better marketing allocation.
